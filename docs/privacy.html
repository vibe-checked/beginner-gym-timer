Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Overview

Beginner Gym Timer does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data. The app is designed as a local-only gym rest timer.

Local App Data

Beginner Gym Timer may store workout edits and active workout session state locally on your device. This data is used only to keep the app working and is not sent to Mark Utility Labs or any third party.

Notifications

The app may use local notifications to alert you when a rest period is complete. These notifications are scheduled on your device. Beginner Gym Timer does not use remote push notifications for marketing or tracking.

Analytics And Tracking

Beginner Gym Timer does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, account creation, or cross-app tracking.
