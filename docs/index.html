What It Does

It removes the setup before the workout.

Beginner Gym Timer gives you simple built-in workout cards, tells you what set you are on, times your rest, and points you to the next set. There is no weight form, no rep form, no history dashboard, no account, and no pressure to turn a quick workout into data entry.

Why It Exists

Working out is already hard. The app should not make it harder.

A beginner can lose momentum before the first set: choosing exercises, entering numbers, wondering whether they should track everything, and trying to understand an app while standing in the gym. Beginner Gym Timer is built for that moment. It keeps the phone out of the way so the next action is obvious: do the set, rest, continue.

The Story

For the person who almost went home.

Imagine someone new walking into the gym after a long day. They want to train, but they are tired, the equipment looks unfamiliar, and every fitness app seems to ask for a plan before they even start. How many sets? How much weight? Which exercise? Are we tracking progress forever?

Beginner Gym Timer is the opposite of that. Open the app, pick a beginner workout, and start. The app does just enough to keep the session moving. No excuse, no spreadsheet, no complicated setup. Just one set at a time.

Motivation

Make the first step smaller.

Consistency often starts with lowering friction. If the workout is the hard part, the technology should be the easy part. Beginner Gym Timer is designed to help you begin immediately, especially when you are not sure where to start.
