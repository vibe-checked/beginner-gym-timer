Support

How can we help?

Beginner Gym Timer is built to get out of your way: open it, pick a workout, and train. Below are answers to the questions people ask most. If yours isn't here, email us and we'll get back to you within a couple of business days.

How do I start a workout?
Open the app, pick a built-in workout card, and start. The app tells you what set you're on, times your rest, and points you to the next set — no setup required.
My rest alerts aren't showing — what should I check?
Make sure notifications are enabled for Gym Timer in iOS Settings → Notifications → Gym Timer. On iPhone you may also need to expand the notification to see its actions. Rest alerts are scheduled locally on your device.
Can I add my own weights and reps?
No — and that's intentional. The app deliberately skips weight forms, rep logs and progress dashboards so you can start training immediately instead of doing data entry in the gym.
Do I need an account?
No. There's no sign-up, no login, and no profile. Just open the app and go.
Is my data sent anywhere?
No. Beginner Gym Timer is local-only. Any workout edits or active session state are stored on your device and are never sent to us or any third party.
Does it cost anything?
No. The app is free, with no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or ads.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy, simply explained

Last updated: May 4, 2026. This policy applies to the Beginner Gym Timer iOS application published by Mark Utility Labs.

🔒

Short version: Beginner Gym Timer is a local-only app. It does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data. There is no account, no analytics, and no tracking.

1. Overview

Beginner Gym Timer does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data. It is designed as a local-only gym rest timer that works entirely on your device.

2. Local app data

The app may store workout edits and active workout session state locally on your device. This data is used only to keep the app working — for example, to remember which set you're on — and is never sent to Mark Utility Labs or any third party. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

3. Notifications

The app may use local notifications to alert you when a rest period is complete. These are scheduled on your device. Beginner Gym Timer does not use remote push notifications for marketing or tracking. You can turn notifications off at any time in iOS Settings.

4. What is NOT collected

Beginner Gym Timer does not include:

  • Analytics or telemetry of any kind.
  • Advertising SDKs or advertising identifiers.
  • Account creation or login.
  • Cross-app or cross-site tracking.
  • Any network requests that send your data off the device.

5. Permissions

The only system capability the app asks for is notifications, used solely for rest alerts. The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, photo library, or health data.

6. Children's privacy

Beginner Gym Timer is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children under 13. Because the app collects no personal data at all, no such data is gathered from any user.

7. Changes to this policy

If material changes are made, the "Last updated" date above will change. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
