About The App

Beginner Gym Timer is a local-only gym rest timer. It does not require an account, does not log weights or reps, and does not collect personal data.

Notifications

If rest alerts are not appearing, check that notifications are enabled for Gym Timer in iOS Settings. Local notification actions may require expanding the notification on iPhone.
